PhyKep

PhyKep is a simulation that demonstrates the laws of Kepler with the example of earth revolving the sub. It consists of two parts: the first shows the first law of Kepler demonstating that a planet has an elyptic orbit. The second part demonstrates the quantitative postulations of the second law of Kepler that states \( \frac{A_1}{t} = \frac{A_2}{t} \) (in words: the area of the elyptic sector that the planet borders in a given timespan \(t\) is always the same). Base of the simulation is the law of gravitation of Newton.
